# Spinner
The **Spinner** component is intendet to indicate to the user that the app is currently busy.

## Basic
In the most basic version, the spinner just shows a animation and puts itself as overlay over 
its entire own dom node.
<Preview>
  <Story name='Basic' height='150px'>{{
    template: `
      <mui-root-container>
        <mui-spinner></mui-spinner>
      </mui-root-container>`
  }}
  </Story>
</Preview>

## Auto Show Timeout
You can specify an auto show timeout in milliseconds via the `autoShowTimeout` attribute to controll
the delay when the spinner shall be shown. After the timeout the spinner becomes visible. Furthermore
you can controll hiding the spinner with the `hideSpinner` attribute.
<Preview>
  <Story name='Auto Show Timeout' height='150px'>{{
    template: `
      <mui-root-container>
        <mui-spinner [autoShowTimeout]="1000"></mui-spinner>
      </mui-root-container>`
  }}
  </Story>
</Preview>
